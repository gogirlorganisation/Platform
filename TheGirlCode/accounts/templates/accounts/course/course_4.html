No one likes to be disturbed while sleeping. Eve is really tired today and she wants to take a nap. She’s told her mum not to disturb her while she’s sleeping. Once she wakes up, her mother can give her chores as she is no longer sleeping. Her request condition expires or, is terminated, once she wakes up and is no longer sleeping.
This is the basic principle of a While Loop

The while loop tells the computer to do something as long as the condition is met.
The condition is evaluated, and if the condition is true, the code within the block is executed.
This repeats until the condition becomes false.

The syntax for while loop is : while [a condition is True]:
[do something]

a = 0 while a < 10: //this is the condition for the loop.
a = a + 1
print a //the condition keeps getting evaluated till it becomes false.
